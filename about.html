Est. 2012 · Hyderabad

Born in a Clay
Oven in 2012

SPICE ROUTE began when Chef Rajan Reddy returned from Paris with a Michelin-trained palate and an obsession with his grandmother's recipes. He opened a single counter in Banjara Hills with one rule: nothing frozen, nothing rushed.

Today we serve over 2,000 orders daily across Hyderabad, but that rule has never changed. Every dish is still prepared from scratch, over live fire, with the same love as day one.

"Food is not just sustenance — it's the language of love, culture, and memory passed between generations."
